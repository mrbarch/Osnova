Green energy is our way to the future

ABOUT US

The scientific and industrial association "OSNOVA" is a modern look at the solution of current problems in the energy sector of the country, for the sake of preserving the environment.

The young and ambitious team that forms the basis of the company aims to research and introduce into everyday life innovative methods and means of electricity generation that do not emit harmful emissions into the atmosphere.

In this difficult time for us, we could not stand aside and accept the challenge of decarbonization to preserve the natural environment, because this is the direction to which all developed countries of the world strive, and make maximum efforts to preserve the natural environment for us and our future generations.

OUR ACTIVITY

We support the course of the country and the world on decarbonization to improve the ecological situation regarding the environment in which we live. To implement such a necessary and extremely important activity, we are conducting research into potentially possible ways of obtaining ecologically clean energy for domestic and industrial use.

"Decarbonization" means moving away from fossil fuels such as coal, natural gas, or oil as soon as possible to carbon-free and renewable energy sources to halt the progress of climate change. Since the signing of the Paris Climate Agreement in 2015, if not before, many countries and companies around the world have committed to decarbonization.

Due to the burning of fossil energy sources, greenhouse gases are released into the atmosphere, which increases the greenhouse effect. This is a key factor in global warming and, as a result, climate change. If the average global temperature rises by more than 1.5°C compared to pre-industrial times, the planet's climate will change irreversibly, with devastating consequences.

Greenhouse gas emissions must be reduced as soon as possible to achieve the climate goals outlined in the Paris Climate Agreement. Thanks to innovative views, ideas, and knowledge, our young team will make every effort to achieve the set goals and make the energy transition to abandon the use of fossil fuels in the future, where possible.

OUR DECISION

Currently, we are working on creating an innovative technical solution, thanks to which citizens and businesses can be provided with electricity anywhere and anytime, regardless of external factors and weather conditions. At the same time, the generated electricity will be absolutely environmentally friendly, since traditional fossil fuels are not used for its generation, but only natural energy.

To carry out our activities, we plan to use engineering software solutions for 3D modeling of the best companies in the world, which are leaders in the field of software. This will allow us to develop and test new design solutions' effectiveness quickly.

Computer modeling and analysis of physical processes is a necessary modern condition that allows in a short period to develop solutions that benefit consumers and profits for companies, thanks to which companies become better in their field and can create even better solutions to meet the needs of consumers.

Using modern software complexes, consumers get high-quality products at more affordable prices, because companies manage to reduce the costs of expensive prototype development by trial and error, which achieves mutual benefit between companies and consumers. And scientific and technological progress only contributes to this positive interaction.

Using all the resources available to us, our team believes that together we will be able to create enough renewable energy capacity that will be able to provide sufficient electricity production for industry, households, the transport sector, and agriculture, which will work in harmony with nature, for the benefit of all of us.
